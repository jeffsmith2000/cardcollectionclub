About Card Collection Club

Built by collectors, for collectors 🇦🇺

Our Story

Card Collection Club started in 2025 as a side project born from frustration. As Australian Pokemon and One Piece TCG collectors, we were tired of fragmented price data, outdated shop listings, and having to check five different sites just to figure out what a card was worth.

So we built the tool we wished existed — a single platform with live pricing, a comprehensive shop directory covering 55+ stores across Australia and New Zealand, grading intel, and market reports that actually make sense.

What started as a spreadsheet became a scraper, then a website, then a community. Today, Card Collection Club is growing into Australia's go-to resource for serious card collectors.

Our Mission

To give every card collector — from beginners opening their first pack to veterans managing five-figure portfolios — the data, tools, and community they need to collect smarter.

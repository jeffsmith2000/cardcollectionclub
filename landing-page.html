FAQ

Got Questions?

What cards do you cover?
We cover Pokémon TCG, NBA/NFL/MLB sports cards, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Magic: The Gathering, and other major TCGs. Our database spans both singles and sealed products across all major sets.
How fast are drop alerts?
Our monitoring bots check retailer stock every 3–5 seconds. Most members receive alerts within 10–15 seconds of a product going live — fast enough to beat the bots and resellers.
Is grading data accurate?
Our grading data is sourced from PSA, BGS, and CGC population reports, updated daily. Our ROI calculator uses real recent sales data from eBay and other marketplaces to give you accurate profit projections.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard with one click and you won't be charged again. Annual plans are prorated if you cancel early.
Do you ship cards?
No — we're a data and alerts platform, not a marketplace. We help you find the best deals and make smarter decisions, but you buy directly from retailers and sellers yourself.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported. All payments are processed securely with 256-bit encryption.

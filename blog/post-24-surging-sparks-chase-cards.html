Surging Sparks Set List — Every Chase Card Ranked

By Ian N · Feb 16, 2026 · Card Collection Club

Surging Sparks (SV7) dropped and it's one of the strongest Pokemon sets in years. Here's every chase card ranked by collectibility, investment potential, and pure "I need that in my binder" energy.

S-Tier — The Must-Haves

1. Charizard ex SAR (Special Art Rare)

Obviously. The full-art Charizard is THE card of the set. Dynamic art, flames everywhere, the kind of card that makes you stop and stare. $85-100 AUD raw, $250+ graded. This will be a $500+ card in PSA 10 within 2 years.

2. Pikachu ex SAR

Pikachu SARs always hold. The art on this one features an adorable scene that captures why we all fell in love with Pokemon. $60-75 AUD raw. Easy hold.

A-Tier — Excellent Pulls

3. Mewtwo ex SAR

Psychic energy radiating off this card. Mewtwo is an evergreen character and this art does it justice. $40-55 AUD — arguably the most undervalued SAR in the set.

4. Eevee ex SAR

The Eeveelution tax is real. Any Eevee or Eeveelution card carries a premium. This SAR is adorable and sits around $50-65 AUD.

5. Mew ex SAR

Another strong psychic type with gorgeous art. $45-60 AUD. Mew has a loyal collector base that supports the price floor.

B-Tier — Solid Hits

6. Arcanine ex SAR

A fan favourite that doesn't always get the spotlight. Great art, good character appeal. $30-45 AUD.

7. Gardevoir ex SAR

Popular in competitive play AND collecting. Dual demand supports price. $35-50 AUD.

8. Full Art Trainer Cards

The full art supporters are always collectible. Look for female trainers — they consistently command premiums. $20-40 AUD range.

C-Tier — Worth Keeping

9-12. Regular ex Cards

The standard ex cards are playable but won't make you rich. Hold the Charizard ex and Pikachu ex regular versions ($5-15 AUD), trade the rest.

13+. Illustration Rares

The illustration rares (IRs) are beautiful but lower value. Great for binders, less exciting for investment. $3-10 AUD each.

Pull Rates

From community data, expect roughly:

  • 1 SAR per 3-4 booster boxes
  • 1-2 ex cards per booster box
  • 2-3 illustration rares per box
  • Plenty of holos and reverse holos

If you're buying sealed, booster boxes around $160-180 AUD are the sweet spot. ETBs are fun but the pull rates make boxes better value.

Happy hunting. May your pulls be SARs and your centering be perfect. ⚡

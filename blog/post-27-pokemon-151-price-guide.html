Pokemon 151 Price Guide 2026 — What's Your Collection Worth?

By Ian N · Feb 13, 2026 · Card Collection Club

Pokemon 151 (SV3.5) is arguably the best modern set ever printed. Nostalgia-fuelled, beautiful art, and strong demand that hasn't slowed down. Here's what your 151 cards are worth in February 2026.

The Big Three

Charizard ex SAR #199

Raw: $150-180 AUD | PSA 10: $450-550 AUD

The undisputed king of the set. Full-art special art rare with flames. This card has held steady while other modern cards dipped. One of the safest holds in the hobby.

Mew ex SAR #205

Raw: $110-130 AUD | PSA 10: $350-400 AUD

Mew floating through space — one of the most beautiful cards in the entire Pokemon TCG. Strong demand from both collectors and investors.

Mewtwo ex SAR #204

Raw: $80-100 AUD | PSA 10: $280-350 AUD

Undervalued compared to Charizard and Mew. The psychic energy art is stunning and Mewtwo has a massive fanbase. Could be the sleeper of the set.

Illustration Rares — Top Picks

  • Alakazam ex IR #201: $25-35 AUD — clean art, nostalgic pull
  • Venusaur ex IR #200: $30-40 AUD — Kanto starter tax
  • Blastoise ex IR #202: $28-38 AUD — completing the trio
  • Erika's Invitation #203: $60-80 AUD — trainer collector favourite
  • Bulbasaur IR #166: $15-20 AUD — adorable, cheap entry point

Regular ex Cards

  • Charizard ex: $15-25 AUD
  • Mew ex: $10-18 AUD
  • Mewtwo ex: $8-15 AUD
  • Alakazam ex: $5-10 AUD

Sealed Product Values

  • Booster Box (JP): $140-160 AUD — still the best value for ripping
  • ETB (EN): $80-100 AUD — already above retail
  • Booster Bundle (EN): $55-70 AUD
  • UPC (EN): $200-250 AUD — if you can find one, grab it

Is 151 Still Worth Buying?

Absolutely. The set has proven staying power because it taps into Gen 1 nostalgia — the same demographic that has disposable income now. Sealed product is getting harder to find at retail, which means prices on both sealed and singles will continue to climb.

If you're building a long-term collection, 151 cards should be in it.

Prices based on eBay AU sold listings, Feb 2026. Markets move — always check current comps before buying or selling. 📊

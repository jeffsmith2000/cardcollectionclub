Streaming Influence: Logan Paul, Pokerev, and others brought mainstream attention

Singles Advantages

Grading Strategy: Maximizing Returns

Professional grading grading has become essential for investment-grade cards. Understanding population reports and grading standards is crucial for maximizing returns.

PS Population Thresholds for Investment

  • PSA 9-10: Base set cards, modern chase cards
  • PSA 8-9: Vintage holos (1999-2003)
  • PSA 9: Error cards and rarities
  • Raw to PSA 9+: Target 20-30% success rate for profit

CGC vs PSA vs BGS (2026 Market Analysis)

Grading Service Comparison

PSA: Premium pricing, highest liquidity, 15% premium in Australia

CGC: Cost-effective, growing acceptance, 8-12% discount to PSA

BGS: Superior for high-grade vintage, 5-8% premium for BGS 9.5+

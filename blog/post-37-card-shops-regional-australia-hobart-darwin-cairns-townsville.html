Card Shops in Regional Australia: Hobart, Darwin, Cairns & Townsville

March 31, 2026 · 9 min read · Card Collection Club AU

Finding card shops outside Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane can feel like hunting for a chase card in a bulk bin. But regional Australia has a growing TCG scene, and these shops are the backbone of it. Here's every dedicated card shop we've found in Hobart, Darwin, Cairns, and Townsville.

Hobart, Tasmania

Tasmania's capital has a small but dedicated card community. Two main shops serve the area, both running regular events.

Good Games Hobart

44 Brisbane St, Hobart TAS 7000

The flagship Hobart card shop. Good Games is a national chain with a strong local presence. They stock Pokemon, One Piece, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh, and more. Weekly tournaments for most major TCGs. Decent play space for in-store events.

Pokemon TCG One Piece TCG Weekly Events Singles Sealed Play Space

Area52

Hobart, TAS

A community hub for card games in Hobart. Specialises in Pokemon with regular tournaments and ample play space. Known for a welcoming atmosphere for new players. Also carries MTG and other TCGs. They sell singles online via their website.

Pokemon TCG Tournaments Play Space Online Singles

Hobart tip: With only two main shops, stock can move fast on new releases. Pre-order sealed product or be there on release day. Both shops have active Facebook groups for event announcements.

Darwin, Northern Territory

The Top End has fewer options but the scene is growing. Darwin's tropical climate means extra care with card storage — humidity is a real threat to card condition.

Games Shed (formerly Hobby Hub)

303/28 Bishop St, Woolner NT 0820

Darwin's primary TCG destination. Specialises in trading card games including One Piece and Pokemon. Previously known as Hobby Hub. Stocks sealed product and runs events. The go-to spot for Darwin collectors.

One Piece TCG Pokemon TCG Events Sealed Product

More Than Meeples

Darwin, NT (online-first with local pickup)

An online board game and card game store based in Darwin. Ships Australia-wide. Stocks collectible card games including Japanese Pokemon cards and One Piece Card Game products. Good option if you prefer online ordering with local pickup.

Japanese Pokemon One Piece TCG Online Store AU-Wide Shipping

Comics NT

Near Casuarina Square, Darwin NT

A comics and collectibles shop that carries Pokemon cards alongside their main comic book stock. Not a dedicated TCG shop, but worth checking for packs and sealed product when you're in the area.

Pokemon TCG Comics Casual Stock

Darwin Storage Warning

Darwin's humidity can warp cards and promote mould. Store your collection in air-conditioned rooms with silica gel packets. Never leave cards in a car or shed. Consider a dehumidifier for your card storage area.

Cairns, Queensland

Far North Queensland's largest city has a surprisingly active card scene, bolstered by tourism bringing visiting collectors through.

The Wicked Goblin

Cairns, QLD

Cairns' main TCG hub. Carries a wide range of trading card games with Pokemon being a major focus. Active local community. Runs events and provides play space. The first stop for any card collector in Cairns.

Pokemon TCG Events Play Space Community Hub

Waru Collectables

Cairns, QLD (physical shop opening 2026)

A hobby and trading card specialist focused on Pokemon. Currently online with a physical shop front opening in Cairns during 2026. Stocks sealed Pokemon TCG products including Japanese releases. Worth following for opening announcements.

Pokemon TCG Japanese Pokemon Sealed Product Online + Physical

Reefside Games Cairns

Cairns, QLD

Stocks trading cards and board games. Offers pre-orders for upcoming Pokemon TCG releases so you can lock in new sets before they drop. Also carries a range of accessories and supplies.

Zing Pop Culture — Cairns Central

Cairns Central Shopping Centre, Cairns QLD

The national chain carries both Pokemon and One Piece TCG products. Not a specialist shop but a reliable source for sealed product, especially new releases and ETBs. Convenient central location.

Townsville, Queensland

North Queensland's second city punches above its weight with a couple of dedicated TCG shops serving the region.

Monster Collector

Townsville, QLD

A dedicated Pokemon TCG and accessories destination. Also stocks Dragon Ball Super, Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, and Weiss Schwarz. Strong focus on providing the full TCG experience with accessories and supplies alongside cards.

Pokemon TCG Dragon Ball Super Yu-Gi-Oh Accessories

NQ Cards & Collectables

Burdell, Townsville QLD

Covers both Pokemon TCG and One Piece TCG with a strong focus on both. One of the few shops in North Queensland with dedicated One Piece stock. Worth the visit if you're collecting either (or both) games.

Online Alternatives for Regional Collectors

When local options are limited, these Australian online retailers ship anywhere in the country:

  • Leo Games — leogames.com.au — Wide range, good prices, fast shipping
  • Mint Collectables — mintcollectables.com.au — Strong Pokemon and One Piece stock
  • TCGroup AU — tcgroupau.com.au — Good for pre-orders and new releases
  • Mayhem Collectables — mayhemcollectables.com.au — Competitive pricing
  • Odyssey TCG — odysseytcg.com.au — Reliable AU retailer
  • JoJos Hobbyhouse — jojoshobbyhouse.com.au — Hobby and TCG specialist
  • Comic Book Factory — comicbookfactory.com.au — Good One Piece TCG stock

Shipping tip: Many online AU retailers offer free shipping over $100-$150. Batch your orders to save on postage. Pre-ordering also typically guarantees allocation on high-demand products that sell out in stores.

Growing the Scene

Regional card communities are built by people who show up. If your city doesn't have a regular tournament scene, talk to your local shop about starting one. Most shops are happy to host if there's demand — they just need someone to help organise it.

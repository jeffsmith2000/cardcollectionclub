AU & NZ Card Events Calendar: Q2-Q3 2026

April 4, 2026 · 7 min read · Card Collection Club

The Australian and New Zealand card convention scene is absolutely stacked for the next six months. From massive trade expos to official Pokemon Regionals to brand new events in cities that have never had a card show before, there's something happening almost every weekend. Here's your complete guide.

April 2026

APR 25-27

Armageddon Expo Auckland (Autumn)

Location: Auckland, New Zealand

Games: One Piece, Pokemon, Pop Culture

Major NZ pop culture expo. Official One Piece Card Game demo sessions with promo cards available. Great for exposure to the NZ collector market.

APR 26

Hobby Hangout XL Melbourne

Location: Royal Exhibition Building, Carlton VIC

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Sports Cards

Massive event with 400+ tables. Live breaks, dedicated trading zones, free play areas. One of the biggest single-day card events in Australia. If you're in Melbourne, this is unmissable.

May 2026

MAY 2-3

Brisbane Card Show

Location: Distillery Road Market, Brisbane QLD

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, MTG, Sports

Two-day event with vendors, grading services on-site, tournaments, and a dedicated trading zone. Brisbane's card scene has exploded and this show reflects it.

brisbanecardshow.com.au

MAY 9-10

CollectFest Brisbane

Location: Brisbane Convention & Exhibition Centre

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Sports, Collectibles

Part of the national CollectFest series (Sydney and Melbourne already done). Major collectibles expo covering cards, comics, toys, and more.

collectfest.com.au

MAY 10

One Piece Treasure Cup Brisbane

Location: Brisbane QLD

Games: One Piece TCG

Official Bandai Treasure Cup tournament. Oceania region. Promo cards and prizes for competitors.

MAY 22-24

Melbourne Pokemon Regional Championships 2026

Location: Melbourne Convention & Exhibition Centre

Games: Pokemon TCG, VGC, Pokemon GO

Official Pokemon Regionals! Championship Points towards Worlds 2026. Major competitive event with side events for collectors. Registrations opened March 25.

Another official Bandai Treasure Cup in Sydney's south. Great for NSW One Piece players.

June 2026

TCG Expo Melbourne

Location: Malvern Town Hall, Melbourne VIC

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Dragon Ball Z, Yu-Gi-Oh

Premier TCG-focused event. Vendor stalls, live events, showcases. More intimate than the mega expos but laser-focused on card games.

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Sports Cards

200+ tables of collectible trading cards in western Sydney. Buy, trade, and compete. Growing event that's becoming a staple for NSW collectors.

Mid-year One Piece event in Auckland. If you're in NZ and play One Piece competitively, mark this one.

August 2026

Sydney Trading Card Show Hurstville

Location: The Auditorium, Club Central, Hurstville NSW

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Sports Cards

Specialized trading card show with vendors, giveaways, prizes, and competitions. Hurstville is becoming a regular venue for Sydney card events.

Adelaide Trading Card Show (Inaugural!)

Location: Adelaide, SA

Games: Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Sports Cards

The first ever dedicated trading card show in Adelaide! SA collectors finally get their own event. Details TBC but this is one to watch.

Tips for Convention Success

  • Bring cash. Many vendors prefer cash and offer discounts for it.
  • Know your prices. Check TCGPlayer and eBay sold listings before you go. Convention pricing can be hit or miss.
  • Arrive early. The best deals go in the first hour. VIP/early bird tickets are usually worth it.
  • Bring trade binders. Trading zones are goldmines if you have the right cards.
  • Set a budget. Convention FOMO is real. Decide what you're hunting before you walk in.

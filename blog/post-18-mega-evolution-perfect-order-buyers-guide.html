Pokemon Mega Evolution Perfect Order - What to Buy in Australia

Published April 1, 2026 | by Card Collection Club

Mega Evolution Perfect Order (ME03) dropped on March 27, 2026, and it's already making waves. The third expansion in the Mega Evolution era brings back fan-favourite Mega Pokemon with stunning new Illustration Rare artwork. Here's your complete AU buyer's guide.

What's in the Set?

Perfect Order continues the Mega Evolution block with over 190 cards, including:

  • New Mega Evolution ex cards including Mega Meganium, Mega Feraligatr, and Mega Emboar
  • Full-art Tyrunt promo in the Elite Trainer Box
  • Illustration Rare and Special Art Rare cards with premium artwork
  • Trainer cards featuring classic characters in new scenarios

Products Available and AU Pricing

Elite Trainer Box - $79.95 AUD RRP

The best value entry point. Includes 11 booster packs, the full-art Tyrunt promo, dice, sleeves, and storage box. The Pokemon Center exclusive ETB has alternate artwork but typically costs more when imported.

Booster Box (36 packs) - $179.95 AUD RRP

Best for ripping or investing. 36 packs gives you the best pull rate odds. Most AU retailers are stocking these at or near RRP right now.

Booster Bundle (6 packs) - $34.95 AUD RRP

Casual option. Good if you want a taste without committing to a full box.

Build & Battle Box - ~$39.95 AUD RRP

Includes 4 packs and a pre-release promo. These are popular with competitive players.

Chase Cards to Watch

Based on early market data and the Japanese set:

  • Mega Meganium ex SAR - The starter Pokemon mega evolutions are driving serious demand
  • Mega Emboar ex SAR - Fire-type collectors love this one
  • Trainer Gallery cards - Always hold value long-term
  • Illustration Rare full arts - The premium artwork cards from this set are exceptional
Investor Tip: The 30th Anniversary hype is real. Sealed Perfect Order product at RRP is likely to appreciate as 2026 anniversary celebrations drive interest in everything Pokemon. If you can grab booster boxes at $170-180, stash a few.

Where to Buy at RRP in Australia

  • EB Games / Zing - Usually has stock at RRP for a few weeks post-release
  • Big W / Kmart / Target - Check weekly. They restock unpredictably but always at RRP
  • Good Games - Local game stores often have competitive pricing
  • Pokemon Center AU - Official online store, ships within AU
  • Your local card shop - Support local! Check our Card Shop Directory to find one near you
AU Market Note: Unlike Prismatic Evolutions (which went to 3-4x RRP), Perfect Order supply seems healthy so far. Don't panic buy at inflated prices. Wait for restocks.

Should You Invest?

The Mega Evolution block has strong nostalgia appeal, and Perfect Order is the third set, which historically is when sets start getting printed less. Sealed booster boxes at RRP are a solid hold for 12-24 months. Singles from this set should be bought after the initial hype dies down (give it 4-6 weeks).

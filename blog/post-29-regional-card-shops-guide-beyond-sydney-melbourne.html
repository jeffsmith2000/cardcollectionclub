Best Card Shops Outside Sydney and Melbourne: Your 2026 Regional Guide

March 30, 2026 · 11 min read · Card Collection Club AU

Everyone knows the big city shops. Good Games Sydney, Cherry Collectables Melbourne, the usual suspects. But if you live outside the capital cities, or you're travelling and want to check out local stores, where do you go?

We've been building Australia's most comprehensive card shop directory at Card Collection Club, and we've found some absolute gems in regional areas. Here's our guide to the best card shops across regional Australia.

Why Shop Local?

Beyond the obvious "support small business" angle, there are real practical reasons to visit regional card shops:

  • Better prices on singles: Regional stores often price below Sydney/Melbourne market rates because demand is lower. You can find bargains.
  • Less competition for new releases: Capital city stores sell out of hot products in minutes. Regional stores sometimes still have stock days later.
  • Community: Smaller stores build tighter communities. You'll actually get to know the staff and other collectors. That leads to better trades and tips on what's coming.
  • Convention prep: Local stores are the best place to hear about upcoming regional events and card shows that don't get advertised on the big socials.

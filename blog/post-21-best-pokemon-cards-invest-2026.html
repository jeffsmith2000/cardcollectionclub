Best Pokemon Cards to Invest In 2026

By Ian N · Feb 19, 2026 · Card Collection Club

The Pokemon TCG market in 2026 is wild. Between Surging Sparks, Prismatic Evolutions hype, and the vintage boom showing no signs of slowing — there's serious money to be made if you know where to look.

Here are my top 10 picks for cards that should appreciate this year.

1. Charizard ex SAR — Surging Sparks

Currently sitting around $85-100 AUD raw. The full-art treatment on this card is next level, and Charizard never loses demand. PSA 10s are already pushing $250+. Buy raw under $90, grade it, thank me later.

2. Umbreon VMAX Alt Art — Evolving Skies

The "Moonbreon" is the modern holy grail. Raw copies hover around $320-400 AUD but PSA 10s regularly clear $800+. Supply is only going down as more get locked in slabs. This is a long-term hold.

3. Pikachu VMAX Rainbow — Vivid Voltage

The thicc Pikachu. Always in demand, especially PSA 10. Currently $280-310 AUD raw. This card has proven floor — it bounces back from every dip.

4. Shanks Manga Art — OP01

One Piece is the fastest-growing TCG in the world right now. The Shanks manga art from OP01 is THE chase card — $400+ AUD and climbing. If you don't own one, you're missing out.

5. Luffy Gear 5 Leader — OP05

Playable AND collectible. The Gear 5 art captures the best moment in One Piece history. $165 AUD and it's a steal at that price. Expect $250+ by end of year.

6. Mew ex SAR — Pokemon 151

151 is one of the best modern sets ever printed. Mew's special art rare is gorgeous and sits around $120 AUD. As sealed product dries up, singles will climb.

7. Rayquaza VMAX Alt Art — Evolving Skies

Another Evolving Skies banger. The gold dragon flying through the atmosphere — pure art. $180-220 AUD raw. Will be a $500+ card in PSA 10 within 2 years.

8. Nami OP01 Alt Art

The most iconic female card in One Piece TCG. Controversial, sure, but the market doesn't lie — $85+ AUD and consistently one of the most searched cards online.

9. Mewtwo ex SAR — Pokemon 151

Underrated pick. Mewtwo is an evergreen character and the 151 SAR treatment is stunning. $42 AUD raw right now — massive upside if you grade it. Buy the dip.

10. Yamato SEC — OP04

OP04 was slept on. The Yamato secret rare has one of the best art pieces in the entire One Piece TCG. Currently $88 AUD — I think this doubles within 12 months as supply tightens.

The Strategy

The play is simple: buy raw, grade the best condition copies, hold the PSA 10s. The spread between raw and graded is where the real money lives. Cards that are $80 raw can be $300+ in a PSA 10 slab.

Focus on cards with strong character appeal (Charizard, Pikachu, Luffy, Shanks), limited print runs, and alt art/SAR treatments. These are the cards that hold and grow value over time.

Not financial advice — but if you're going to spend money on cardboard, at least make it the right cardboard. 🔥

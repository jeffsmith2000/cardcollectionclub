One Piece OP-15: Adventure on Kami's Island - Everything You Need to Know

Published April 1, 2026 | by Card Collection Club

The Skypiea arc finally gets its time in the One Piece Card Game. OP-15 / EB-04 "Adventure on Kami's Island" drops April 3, 2026, and it's a unique "double-stuffed" hybrid set that combines a main booster and extra booster in one release. Here's the full breakdown for AU collectors.

What Makes This Set Special?

This is Bandai's first hybrid set - combining OP-15 and EB-04 into one release. That means:

  • 7 new Leaders - the most new Leaders in any single release
  • Skypiea arc focus - Enel, Wiper, Gan Fall, and the Shandians get their moment
  • Double card pool - More cards per set than usual, meaning more chase cards but also more to collect
  • Pre-release events: March 27 - April 2, 2026 at participating stores across AU

Chase Cards

Based on the Japanese release and early reveals:

  • Enel (Leader) - The God of Skypiea as a Leader card. Expect high demand.
  • Nico Robin alt-art - Heroines edition alt-arts have been consistently expensive
  • Wiper SEC - Shandian warrior Secret Rare
  • Nami (Skypiea) - Popular character + arc-specific artwork = demand

AU Pricing

  • Booster Box (24 packs): ~$139.95 AUD RRP
  • Double Pack Set DP-10: Value pack with bonus DON!! card - great for casual buyers
Pre-release Tip: Check your local card shop for pre-release events running March 27 to April 2. You get early access to the set, a promo card, and the community experience. Find shops near you in our Card Shop Directory.

Investment Perspective

One Piece TCG has been on a tear. The game is growing faster than any other TCG in Australia, and early sets like OP01 Romance Dawn are already commanding serious premiums (see our price tracker). Here's why OP-15 matters:

  • Skypiea is beloved - This arc has a dedicated fanbase who've been waiting for this set
  • Hybrid format is new - First of its kind, which could make sealed product more desirable long-term
  • 7 Leaders - Competitive players need these, driving sustained demand
Buy Strategy: Grab 1-2 boxes at RRP for ripping. If the set performs well (check Japanese secondary market as a leading indicator), consider holding a sealed box. One Piece sealed has been one of the best performing TCG investments over the past 18 months.

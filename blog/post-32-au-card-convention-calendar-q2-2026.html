Convention Prep Checklist

Whether you're buying, selling, or trading, here's how to prepare:

For Buyers

  • Set a budget. Bring cash. Many vendors prefer it and some offer cash discounts.
  • Know your prices. Check TCGPlayer/eBay sold prices the night before. Don't overpay in the excitement.
  • Bring a trade binder. Even if you're mainly buying, you might find better value through trades.
  • Arrive early. The best deals go in the first hour. Especially for vintage and graded cards.

For Sellers/Vendors

  • Stock what's hot. Right now: Prismatic Evolutions singles, OP15 chase cards (post-April 3), Mega Evolution products.
  • Price competitively. Convention buyers are savvy. They'll check their phones. Be at or below TCGPlayer market.
  • Bring variety. Mix of sealed product, singles, and graded cards draws the widest audience.
  • Display matters. Clean binders, sorted cards, clear pricing. Don't make people dig through boxes unless that's your angle (and price accordingly).

For Traders

  • Bring your trade binder with clear "for trade" marking
  • Know your values and be fair - the community is small in AU
  • Specialise - if you're the "One Piece alt art guy" or the "vintage holo trader", people will find you

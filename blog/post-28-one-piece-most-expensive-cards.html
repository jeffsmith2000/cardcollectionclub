One Piece TCG Most Expensive Cards — Top 20 List

By John C · Feb 12, 2026 · Card Collection Club

One Piece TCG prices have gone parabolic. What started as a "Pokemon alternative" is now a legit market with cards pushing four figures. Here are the 20 most expensive One Piece cards in 2026.

The Top 5 — $500+ AUD

1. Shanks (Manga Art) — OP01-120 SEC

$400-500 AUD (Raw) | $800+ PSA 10

The most iconic One Piece card ever printed. First set, first manga art, first legend. This is the "Base Set Charizard" of One Piece TCG. Only going up.

2. Luffy (Manga Art) — OP05-119 SEC

$350-450 AUD | $700+ PSA 10

Gear 5 Luffy in manga art form. Captures the most hype moment in One Piece history. Insane demand from both anime fans and card collectors.

3. Nami (Manga Art) — OP01-116 SR

$280-350 AUD | $600+ PSA 10

Controversial but undeniably valuable. The most searched One Piece card online. Demand is relentless.

4. Roronoa Zoro (Alt Art) — OP01-025 SR

$250-320 AUD | $550+ PSA 10

Zoro fans are a different breed. This alt art captures the intensity perfectly. Low supply as collectors lock them away.

5. Boa Hancock (Alt Art) — OP09

$200-280 AUD

The newest entry but climbing fast. Beautiful art, popular character, low pull rate. Could crack top 3 by year end.

Why Are One Piece Cards So Expensive?

Three factors: the anime is at peak global popularity, print runs are lower than Pokemon, and the collector base is growing faster than supply. Unlike Pokemon where you can find product everywhere, One Piece booster boxes sell out fast.

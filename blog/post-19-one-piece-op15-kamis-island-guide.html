One Piece OP15: Adventure on Kami's Island - Set Preview & What to Buy

Published March 27, 2026 | By Card Collection Club

OP15 pre-release events kick off this weekend (March 29-30) and the full English release drops April 3. The Sky Island arc gets its moment in the One Piece Card Game, and if the Japanese release is any indicator, this set has some serious heat.

Key Dates

  • Japanese Release: February 28, 2026 (already out)
  • English Pre-Release Events: March 29-30, 2026
  • English Full Release: April 3, 2026
  • Booster Box: 24 packs, 12 cards per pack

What is the Theme?

OP15 covers the beloved Sky Island arc (Jaya + Skypiea), alongside characters from Dressrosa and East Blue. This means we are getting some of the most iconic One Piece moments in card form - God Enel, Wyper, the Shandians, and of course the Straw Hat crew in their Skypiea outfits.

Chase Cards from the Japanese Set

Based on JP OP15 data, these are the cards collectors are hunting:

  • Enel (Secret Rare) - God Enel is one of the most popular One Piece villains. His SEC is the headline chase card of the set.
  • Nami (Sky Island Alt Art) - Fan-favourite character in Skypiea attire. Expect premium pricing.
  • Monkey D. Luffy (Leader - Alt Art) - Luffy leaders always command a premium, and the Sky Island version is no exception.
  • Wyper (Super Rare) - The Shandian warrior gets his moment. Strong competitive potential.

Market Context: One Piece TCG is on Fire

The One Piece Card Game market has been on a tear. Here is what you need to know:

  • OP01 Romance Dawn (Wave 1): ~$8,000-9,400 AUD per booster box. Absolute trophy asset.
  • OP01 Romance Dawn (Wave 2): ~$2,500-2,600 AUD per booster box.
  • Player base: Has grown dramatically since 2022 launch. Oceania Regionals in March 2026 drew massive numbers.
  • Sealed appreciation: Almost every OP set from OP01-OP10 has appreciated above RRP.

Buying Strategy for AU Collectors

Pre-Release Weekend (Mar 29-30)

If your local store is running pre-release sealed events, go. You get early access to the set and the sealed experience is always fun. Check Good Games, Card Merchant, and local TCG stores for event listings.

Release Week (Apr 3+)

Booster boxes at RRP are the play. One Piece sealed has consistently appreciated, so buying at retail is almost always a winning move if you can hold 6-12 months.

Singles Strategy

Wait 1-2 weeks after release for singles prices to settle. The initial hype inflates SEC/SR prices by 20-40%. Patient buyers win.

Where to Find OP15 in Australia

Pre-orders are live at most major TCG retailers. Check your local store first to support the community, then look online:

  • Good Games stores (nationwide)
  • Card Merchant (NZ)
  • Collector stores online
  • eBay AU for competitive pricing post-release

Bottom Line

OP15 is a strong set riding the momentum of a red-hot One Piece TCG market. Sky Island is one of the most beloved arcs, and the chase cards reflect that. At RRP, sealed booster boxes are a solid pick-up. For the Enel SEC hunters - patience pays.

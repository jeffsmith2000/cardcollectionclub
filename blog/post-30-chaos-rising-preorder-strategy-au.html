Mega Evolution Chaos Rising: Preorder Strategy for AU Collectors

Published April 3, 2026 | Card Collection Club

Mega Evolution — Chaos Rising drops May 22, 2026, and it's shaping up to be one of the biggest Pokemon TCG releases of the year. Mega Greninja ex headlines the set with 120+ cards, and early JP data from the parallel "Abyss Eye" set suggests some serious chase cards.

Here's exactly how to position yourself as an Australian collector.

What's in Chaos Rising?

  • 120+ cards including new Mega Pokemon ex
  • Mega Greninja ex — the star card, expected to be highly sought after
  • Full Art Fennekin promo in ETBs
  • Pokemon Center exclusive ETB with metallic Mega Greninja promo
  • Build & Battle Boxes for pre-release events (4 unique promos)

Where to Preorder in Australia

Best Value (Ship AU-wide)

  • Gameology — consistent pricing, fast dispatch. One of the most reliable AU retailers.
  • EB Games / Zing — RRP pricing, widely accessible. Good for PC ETBs.
  • Good Games — Build & Battle events + sealed. Check your local store.
  • Card Merchant NZ — competitive NZD pricing, ships to AU quickly.

Local Game Stores Worth Checking

  • ACE Collectibles (Adelaide) — strong preorder pricing
  • Urban Empire (Newcastle) — often below RRP
  • Misty's Collectables (Brisbane) — good allocation, reliable
  • Just Da Best Cards (Perth) — growing store with competitive prices

Investment Strategy

Short-Term Flip (0-3 months)

  • Target: Pokemon Center ETBs and Build & Battle Boxes
  • PC ETBs always command a premium post-release due to limited supply
  • Build & Battle promos can spike if the right card hits competitive play

Long-Term Hold (12+ months)

  • Target: Sealed Booster Boxes — Mega Evolution era boxes are ageing well
  • Greninja is a fan favourite with massive cross-generational appeal
  • Compare to Prismatic Evolutions: RRP $79.95 ETB now sells for $400+ sealed

Budget Play

  • Grab 2-3 ETBs at RRP from Big W, Kmart, or Target on launch day
  • Hold sealed for 12 months minimum
  • Realistic 50-100% return if the set has strong chase cards

Why Greninja Matters

Greninja consistently ranks in the top 5 most popular Pokemon worldwide. The original Greninja-GX was a competitive staple and collector favourite. Mega Greninja ex has the potential to be this generation's defining chase card — especially the alt-art version, which JP collectors are already pricing at a premium.

Key Dates

  • Pre-release events: May 10-18 (check your local Good Games or LGS)
  • Full release: May 22, 2026
  • JP "Abyss Eye" launch: May 22 (same day — watch JP pull rates for intel)

Bottom Line

Chaos Rising is a "buy" for both collectors and investors. The Mega Evolution series has been strong across the board, and Greninja's popularity makes this set a standout. Lock in preorders now while prices are still below secondary market expectations.

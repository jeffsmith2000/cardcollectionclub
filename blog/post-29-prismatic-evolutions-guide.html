Prismatic Evolutions — Release Date, Price & What to Expect

By Michael H · Feb 11, 2026 · Card Collection Club

Prismatic Evolutions is the most anticipated Pokemon set of 2025-2026 and it's living up to the hype. Here's everything you need to know about the Eeveelution-focused set that's taken the hobby by storm.

What Is Prismatic Evolutions?

Prismatic Evolutions (SV8a) is a special expansion focused entirely on Eevee and its evolutions. Think of it like a love letter to Eeveelution fans — every Eeveelution gets the premium art treatment with SARs, full arts, and illustration rares.

Release Date & Availability

English release: Available now (released late 2025)

Japanese release: Already out — JP product is available at select Australian retailers and Japanese import shops.

Availability has been challenging. ETBs sold out instantly at most retailers. Restocks happen but you need to be fast. Set up alerts at your local shop or follow Australian card deal accounts on social media.

Chase Cards

The set revolves around Eeveelution SARs. Here are the big hits:

  • Umbreon ex SAR: The crown jewel. $150-200+ AUD. Umbreon tax in full effect.
  • Sylveon ex SAR: $80-120 AUD. Gorgeous fairy aesthetic.
  • Espeon ex SAR: $70-100 AUD. Psychic vibes, undervalued.
  • Glaceon ex SAR: $60-90 AUD. Clean ice-themed art.
  • Leafeon ex SAR: $50-75 AUD. Nature art, very collectible.
  • Flareon/Jolteon/Vaporeon ex SARs: $50-80 AUD each. The OG trio.
  • Eevee ex SAR: $70-100 AUD. The base evolution, surprisingly valuable.

Pricing

  • ETB: $85-110 AUD (above $59.95 RRP — good luck finding retail)
  • Booster Box (JP): $110-140 AUD
  • Booster Bundle: $55-70 AUD
  • Binder Collection: $45-60 AUD

Is It Worth Buying?

At retail prices? Absolutely. The Eeveelution theme gives this set massive broad appeal — casual fans, competitive players, and hardcore collectors all want these cards. The pull rates are decent and the art quality across the board is elite.

At scalper prices ($100+ for an ETB)? Harder call. If you're opening for fun and can afford it, go for it. If you're investing, buy singles instead — the math is better.

Where to Buy in Australia

  • Local card shops — best chance for retail pricing. Check our shop directory
  • Toymate, Zing, Big W — restocks happen randomly
  • EB Games — limited stock, often online only
  • eBay AU — for singles and market-priced sealed

Prismatic Evolutions is a set you'll want to have in your collection long-term. The Eeveelution premium never fades. ✨

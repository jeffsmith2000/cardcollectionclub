Grading Service Comparison Matrix

Choosing the right grading service significantly impacts long-term value and marketability. Each service has distinct advantages and market positioning.

PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator)

  • Market Premium: 15-25% higher value than CGC equivalents
  • Liquidity: Highest demand, fastest sales
  • Population Control: Strict grading standards
  • Best For: Vintage cards, major chase cards, blue-chip investments
  • Processing Time: 45-75 business days (Economy tier)

CGC (Certified Guaranty Company)

  • Market Premium: 10-18% discount to PSA equivalents
  • Cost Advantage: 15% lower submission costs
  • Grading Strictness: Often equal to PSA, sometimes stricter
  • Best For: Modern cards, bulk submissions, arbitrage opportunities
  • Processing Time: 30-45 business days (Economy tier)

BGS (Beckett Grading Services)

  • Market Premium: 5-12% premium for BGS 9.5+ over PSA 9
  • Sub-grading: Provides individual sub-grades for corners, edges, surface
  • Aesthetics: Durable cases with sub-grade detail
  • Best For: High-grade vintage cards, premium modern cards
  • Processing Time: 40-60 business days (Economy tier)
Cost-Benefit Analysis: PSA remains the gold standard but CGC offers superior value for submissions over $50 in raw card value. Factor the 15% market discount into your break-even calculations.

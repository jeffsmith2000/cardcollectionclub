Australia & NZ Card Events Calendar - Q2 2026

Published March 29, 2026 | By Card Collection Club

Q2 2026 is absolutely stacked for card collectors in Australia and New Zealand. From massive conventions to official Pokemon and One Piece tournaments, here is every event worth knowing about from April through June.

April 2026

April 18-19 - Supanova Melbourne

Melbourne Showgrounds, VIC

Australia's biggest pop culture convention. While not card-specific, Supanova always has a strong TCG vendor presence. Great for finding deals on singles and sealed product from indie sellers. Worth the trip if you are in Melbourne.

April 24 - Pokemon Mega Evolution Ascended Heroes New Products

Nationwide (AU retail)

Booster Bundles, Mega Meganium ex Box, Mega Emboar ex Box, and Mega Feraligatr ex Box all drop this date. Hit up Kmart, Big W, or your local game store early.

April 26 - The Hobby Hangout XL

Royal Exhibition Building, Melbourne, VIC

Australia's LARGEST Hobby Hangout. 150+ stalls covering Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, One Piece, sports cards, and more. Live breaks, trading zones, free play areas. This is a must-attend if you are anywhere near Melbourne. Website

May 2026

May 2-3 - TreasureCon II

Perth Convention and Exhibition Centre, WA

Western Australia's biggest TCG and collectibles event returns for two full days. Over 100 vendors with Pokemon, One Piece, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh, sports cards, graded slabs, sealed products, and exclusive show specials. If you are in Perth, this is THE event.

May 2-3 - Brisbane Card Show 2026

Distillery Road Market, Brisbane, QLD

Queensland's newest card show. Trading, tournaments, grading services, and a solid community atmosphere. Great for both Pokemon and sports card collectors. Website

May 10 - One Piece Treasure Cup Brisbane

Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre, QLD

Official Bandai Treasure Cup tournament. Competitive One Piece TCG at its finest. Prize support includes exclusive promo cards and invitations to higher-level events.

May 22 - Pokemon Chaos Rising Release Day

Nationwide (AU retail + game stores)

The Mega Greninja set drops. Expect pre-release events at local game stores the weekend before. Booster boxes, ETBs, and Build & Battle boxes all available.

May 22-24 - Melbourne Pokemon Regional Championships

Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre, VIC

Official Pokemon Regional Championship. Championship Points towards the 2026 World Championships. Side events, exclusive prizes, and the best competitive Pokemon in AU/NZ. Official page

May 24 - One Piece Treasure Cup Hurstville

Alt Events Centre, Hurstville, NSW

Sydney-area Treasure Cup for the One Piece competitive community.

June 2026

June 6-7 - Oz Comic Con Melbourne

Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre, VIC

Major pop culture convention with a strong TCG vendor presence. Good for finding rare singles and sealed product from collectors downsizing.

June 7 - The TCG Expo Melbourne

Malvern Town Hall, Melbourne, VIC

Dedicated TCG event featuring Pokemon, One Piece, Dragon Ball Z, and more. Vendor stalls, live events, showcases. A focused, card-only experience. Website

June 7 - One Piece Treasure Cup Perth

Perth Convention and Exhibition Centre, WA

Official Treasure Cup tournament for Perth's growing One Piece community.

June 12 - One Piece OP16 Paramount War Release

Nationwide

The Marineford set arrives in English. One of the most anticipated One Piece TCG releases of the year. Pre-order early.

June 14 - NZ Card Collectors Show

Te Rauparaha Arena, Porirua, Wellington, NZ

New Zealand's most comprehensive card collecting show. Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, sports cards - everything under one roof. The biggest NZ card event of the year.

June 19-20 - Supanova Sydney

Sydney Showgrounds, NSW

Sydney's turn for Supanova. TCG vendors, anime merch, and pop culture across two days.

June 28 - TCXPO 2026

Oran Park Leisure Centre, NSW

Over 200 tables of collectible trading cards. Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, Lorcana, One Piece. Grading submissions on-site. One of Sydney's biggest dedicated card events. Website

Planning Tips

For Buyers

  • Bring cash. Many vendors offer cash discounts at conventions.
  • Know your prices before you go. Check TCGplayer and eBay sold listings the night before.
  • Arrive early for the best sealed product deals. Late afternoon is better for negotiating singles.

For Sellers

  • Book tables early - the big events sell out months ahead.
  • Price competitively. Convention buyers have dozens of vendors to compare.
  • Bring variety. Sealed product, singles, graded cards, and accessories cover all buyer types.

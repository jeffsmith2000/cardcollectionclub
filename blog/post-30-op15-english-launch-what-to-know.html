OP15 Adventure on Kami's Island: English Launch Guide for AU Collectors

March 31, 2026 · 7 min read · Card Collection Club AU

OP15 drops in English on April 3, 2026, and the pre-release events have been running all weekend. If you're an AU collector wondering whether to crack boxes or hold sealed, here's everything you need to make a smart decision.

What's in the Set?

Adventure on Kami's Island covers the Skypiea arc, one of the most beloved story arcs in One Piece. Expect Enel, Gan Fall, Wiper, and of course, Luffy in some standout artwork.

  • Set code: OP15
  • Pack count: 24 packs per box, 12 cards per pack
  • Key chase cards: Monkey D. Luffy SEC (OP15-119), Monkey D. Luffy SEC Alt Art (OP15-119), Enel Leader
  • New mechanics: Updated Leader Cards with unique effects

Box Pricing in Australia

AU retailers have been listing OP15 booster boxes at $145-165 AUD, which is standard One Piece pricing. If you can get boxes under $150, that's a solid buy.

  • Good Games: $159.95 AUD (in-store and online)
  • eBay AU pre-orders: $145-170 AUD depending on seller
  • Japanese boxes: Still available around $90-110 AUD (JP version released earlier)

Pro tip: Japanese OP15 boxes have been available for weeks, so the JP singles market has already settled. Use JP prices as a floor estimate for where EN singles will end up 2-3 weeks post-release.

Should You Crack or Hold?

Here's the honest take:

  • Crack if: You want to play the set, or you're chasing the Luffy SEC Alt Art for your collection. The pull rates on One Piece are generally decent, and Skypiea is a fan-favourite arc.
  • Hold sealed if: You're looking at 12-18 month appreciation. OP15 has good long-term potential because of the arc popularity and the Luffy chase card.
  • Best of both: Buy 2-3 boxes. Crack 1-2, hold 1 sealed. Classic strategy.

Watch Out For

One Piece sets have been getting heavier print runs lately. OP14 flooded the market within weeks. If OP15 gets similar treatment, sealed box appreciation will be slower. Don't overcommit.

Pre-Release Event Recap

Pre-release events ran March 28-29 across AU. If you attended, you got early access to 3 packs plus a promo card. The buzz has been strong, with multiple stores reporting sell-out events.

OP15 release events continue this week at select stores. Check Good Games events page or your local store for remaining spots.

The Bottom Line

OP15 is a solid set with a beloved arc and a genuine chase card in the Luffy SEC Alt Art. For AU collectors, get your boxes at $150 or under, crack what you want to enjoy, and hold 1-2 sealed if you believe in the long-term One Piece trajectory. The game is growing and Skypiea nostalgia is real.

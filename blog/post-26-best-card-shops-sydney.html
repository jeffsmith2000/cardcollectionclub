Best Card Shops in Sydney — Where to Buy Pokemon & One Piece Cards

By Michael H · Feb 14, 2026 · Card Collection Club

Sydney's card shop scene has exploded over the past two years. Whether you're chasing Japanese exclusives, need singles for your deck, or just want to crack packs — here's where to go.

CBD & Inner City

Good Games Sydney (Town Hall)

The flagship Good Games location. Massive singles wall, regular tournaments, and they run grading group submissions. Open late, easy to get to. Great for after-work pack openings.

Kinokuniya (The Galleries)

Not a card shop per se, but they stock Japanese Pokemon and One Piece sealed product. If you're looking for JP booster boxes at reasonable prices, this is your spot.

Western Sydney

Hobby Emporium (Parramatta)

Solid range of sealed product across Pokemon, One Piece, and Dragon Ball. Good prices and the staff actually know their stuff. Regular locals who trade and hang out.

TCG Emporium (various locations)

Multiple locations across Western Sydney. Good for One Piece singles and they run weekly tournaments. Pricing is competitive with online stores.

North Shore & Northern Beaches

Good Games Chatswood

Strong competitive scene here. If you're looking to play One Piece or Pokemon competitively, the regulars are skilled and welcoming. Weekly events most days.

South & South-West

Games Corner (Hurstville)

One of the best spots for Asian TCGs. Great selection of Japanese product, One Piece, and Pokemon. The area has a strong collecting community.

Online (Sydney-Based)

Cardboard Collectibles

Sydney-based online store with fast shipping. Good for sealed product and they often have pre-order deals. Follow their socials for drop alerts.

Tips for Shopping Local

  • Call ahead for specific singles — most shops have more stock than what's displayed
  • Build relationships — regulars get first dibs on new stock and sometimes better deals
  • Check tournament schedules — playing at events is the best way to meet other collectors
  • Compare prices to eBay AU sold listings before buying expensive singles
  • Support local — these shops are the backbone of the community

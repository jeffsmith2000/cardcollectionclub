PSA Grading Australia — Complete Guide to Getting Your Cards Graded

By Michael H · Feb 17, 2026 · Card Collection Club

PSA grading can turn a $50 card into a $500 card — or cost you $30 to find out your "mint" card is actually a 7. Here's everything Australian collectors need to know about submitting to PSA in 2026.

What Is PSA Grading?

Professional Sports Authenticator (PSA) is the gold standard for card grading. They inspect your card's centering, corners, edges, and surface, then seal it in a tamper-proof slab with a grade from 1-10. A PSA 10 (Gem Mint) is the holy grail.

How Much Does It Cost?

PSA pricing (as of 2026, in USD — roughly 1.55x for AUD):

  • Value tier: ~$25 USD (~$39 AUD) — cards declared under $500 value. 150+ business day turnaround.
  • Regular: ~$50 USD (~$78 AUD) — 65 business day turnaround
  • Express: ~$100 USD (~$155 AUD) — 20 business day turnaround
  • Super Express: ~$200+ USD — 5 business day turnaround

Plus shipping both ways. Budget ~$50-80 AUD total for a value submission including postage.

How to Submit from Australia

  1. Create a PSA account at psacard.com
  2. Choose your service level — Value tier for most cards
  3. Fill out the submission form — card name, set, year, declared value
  4. Package carefully — card savers (NOT toploaders), bubble mailer, tracked shipping
  5. Ship to PSA's US facility — or use a local group submission through your card shop

Pro tip: Many Australian card shops run group submissions. This saves on shipping and they handle the paperwork. Check our shop directory — look for shops that offer grading services.

Which Cards Are Worth Grading?

The golden rule: only grade cards where the PSA 10 value is at least 3x the raw price.

Good candidates in 2026:

  • Charizard ex SAR (Surging Sparks) — Raw ~$90, PSA 10 ~$250+
  • Umbreon VMAX Alt Art — Raw ~$350, PSA 10 ~$850+
  • Shanks Manga Art OP01 — Raw ~$400, PSA 10 ~$800+
  • Any vintage holo (Base Set, Jungle, Fossil) in genuinely mint condition

Cards NOT worth grading: commons, low-value rares, anything with visible whitening or centering issues.

PSA vs CGC vs BGS

PSA commands the highest resale premiums in Australia. If you're grading to sell, PSA is the way. CGC is catching up and often has faster turnaround. BGS (Beckett) is great for the Black Label 10 but has lower liquidity in the AU market.

Turnaround Reality Check

PSA's stated turnarounds are guidelines, not guarantees. Value tier can take 4-8 months from when you ship to when your slabs arrive back in Australia. Factor in 2-3 weeks shipping each way.

Plan ahead. Don't submit cards you might want to sell in the next 6 months unless you're paying for express.

Grade smart, not often. One well-chosen PSA 10 is worth more than ten PSA 8s collecting dust. 💎

Mega Evolution: Perfect Order - Complete Set Guide & Investment Analysis

Published March 27, 2026 | By Card Collection Club

The third Mega Evolution expansion drops today, and Perfect Order is stacked. With over 120 cards featuring Mega Zygarde ex as the headline act, this set continues the Mega Evolution era that has reinvigorated the Pokemon TCG market. Here is everything you need to know.

What is in the Set?

Perfect Order is the third set in the Scarlet & Violet Mega Evolution series. It follows Ascended Heroes (January 2026) and builds on the Mega Evolution mechanic that has driven massive collector interest this year.

Key Products Available

  • Booster Box (36 packs) - RRP ~$220 AUD
  • Elite Trainer Box - RRP ~$80 AUD
  • 3-Pack Blister Bundles - RRP ~$20 AUD
  • Checklane Blisters - RRP ~$8 AUD

Chase Cards to Watch

Based on early Japanese set data and pre-release pulls, these are the cards driving the most hype:

  • Mega Zygarde ex (Special Art Rare) - The cover star. Complete Forme Zygarde art is going to be iconic. Early estimates suggest $80-150+ AUD for the SAR.
  • Mega Zygarde ex (Illustration Rare) - Alternative art version. Expect strong demand from the Kalos collector crowd.
  • Full Art Trainer Supporters - Always chase-worthy. Watch for any fan-favourite characters getting the full art treatment.
  • Gold Rare Energy Cards - Consistent performers that hold value long-term.

Investment Angle: Should You Buy Sealed?

Here is the honest take for Australian collectors:

Buy Now If:

  • You can get booster boxes at or below RRP ($220 AUD)
  • You plan to hold sealed for 12-24 months minimum
  • The Mega Evolution mechanic continues driving competitive play

Wait If:

  • Boxes are marked up above $250 AUD - second wave prints usually bring prices back to RRP
  • You are purely chasing singles - wait 2-3 weeks for the market to settle post-release

AU Market Context

The Australian Pokemon TCG market has been heating up in 2026. Ascended Heroes booster boxes launched at $220 and are already trading at $240-260 sealed. The Mega Evolution era is creating genuine long-term collector demand - not just hype cycles.

For context, Prismatic Evolutions ETBs (released January 2025) are now sitting at ~$250 AUD on the secondary market. That is the kind of appreciation sealed Pokemon products can deliver.

Bottom Line

Perfect Order is a solid set with strong chase cards and competitive-relevant Pokemon. At RRP, sealed is a reasonable hold. For singles hunters, give it 2-3 weeks then snipe the dips. The Mega Evolution era is shaping up to be one of the best periods in modern Pokemon TCG collecting.

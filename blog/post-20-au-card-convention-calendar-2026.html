Australian Card Convention Calendar 2026 - The Complete Guide

Published April 1, 2026 | Updated regularly | by Card Collection Club

2026 is shaping up to be the biggest year ever for card conventions in Australia and New Zealand. From mega shows with 400+ vendor tables to intimate community events, there's something happening almost every month. Here's every major event we're tracking.

Armageddon Expo Wellington (NZ)

Wellington, New Zealand

Pop culture expo with dedicated TCG events area. Great for NZ collectors.

April 25-27

Auckland, New Zealand

Auckland's autumn pop culture event with TCG vendors and trading.

Royal Exhibition Building, Carlton VIC

400+ tables of vendors with Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, sports cards. One of AU's biggest card shows. Don't miss this one.

Convention Tips for Collectors

Bring Cash: Many vendors prefer cash and offer cash discounts. ATMs at conventions often have long queues.
Arrive Early: The best deals go fast. If doors open at 10am, be there at 9:30.
Know Your Prices: Check TCGPlayer, eBay sold listings, and PriceCharting before you go. Knowledge = better deals.
Bring Trade Binders: Conventions are the best place to trade. Organise your binders by game and value tier.
Set a Budget: It's easy to overspend. Decide your max before you walk in.

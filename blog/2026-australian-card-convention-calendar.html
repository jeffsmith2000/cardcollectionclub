Vendor Success Strategies

Application Timing

  • New vendor applications open 10-12 weeks before major conventions
  • Returning vendors get 48-hour early access to table bookings
  • Popular events (CollectFest, Trading Card Summit) fill vendor spots in under 48 hours
  • Regional shows often have tables available 4-6 weeks before event

Inventory Planning

  • Pokemon cards: 60% of sales volume at most conventions
  • One Piece TCG: 25% and growing rapidly
  • Other TCGs (Lorcana, MTG): 15% but higher margins
  • Bring 3x your normal online inventory for major conventions
  • Price competitively - convention shoppers compare aggressively

Regional Market Differences

  • Sydney: High-end collectors, willing to pay premium for rare cards
  • Melbourne: Competitive players, focus on meta-relevant cards
  • Brisbane: Family collectors, sealed products sell well
  • Perth: Limited local options, strong buyer demand
  • Adelaide: Emerging market, less competition but smaller crowds

2026 Pro Tip: Apply for multiple regional shows rather than betting everything on one major convention. Smaller events often provide better ROI per table and let you test different inventory mixes.

2027 Planning

Several organizers have already announced expansion plans for 2027. The Trading Card Summit is adding a Brisbane date, and CollectFest is exploring a Perth launch. Get on vendor mailing lists now for early application access.
